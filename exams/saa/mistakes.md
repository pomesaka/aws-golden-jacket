## Mistake severity

### High severity

Wrong and confident. This means the mental model is actively dangerous.

### Medium severity

Wrong with low confidence. This means the area is weak but not misleading.

### Low severity

Correct but low confidence. This means knowledge exists but needs reinforcement.
